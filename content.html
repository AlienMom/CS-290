Amateur Radio
A hobby that uses radio frequencies to transmit information, locally or over vast differences.

 

DX
In the amateur radio world, DX is an abbreviation for "distance," usually meaning outside of one's home country.

 

Morse Code
This system uses short and long signals to communicate over long distances. It is still in use today.
